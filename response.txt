Sure! Here's a simple recipe:

1. Cream together 1 cup butter, 1 cup sugar, and 1 cup brown sugar.
2. Beat in 2 eggs and 1 tsp vanilla.
3. Mix in 3 cups flour, 1 tsp baking soda, 2 tsp hot water, 1/2 tsp salt.
4. Stir in 2 cups chocolate chips.
5. Drop by spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
6. Bake at 350°F for 10 min. Enjoy!
